ALUMNI



Whether you've just arrived on campus or you’ve bled purple for years, Our Northeastern is the place to connect to all things and all people of Information Systems at Northeastern. Socialize, network, share news, and get involved with fellow alumni, students, parents, faculty, staff, and friends. The University community is more than 200,000 strong, and it comes together through Our Northeastern, an online community free and open to all. Get access to the people, events, ideas, and resources that matter to you, and help strengthen the University. Once you’re a Wildcat, you’re always connected, and it starts here.


Nationwide, there are more than 100 active alumni chapters of the Information Systems. These chapters host interesting/informative university-related events, recruit students to our beautiful campus, and raise funds for local chapter scholarships. Chapter activities range from pregame pep rallies to dinner receptions for IS officials. To recruit students to the University, chapters host Junior Honors programs to recognize outstanding academic achievement, communicate with potential students and scholarship recipients, and attend local college nights as University representatives.
